ENVISAGE

ENVISAGE is a fun event that sticks on to the theme 'EcoTech - Combating Climate Change Using Technological Innovation' which consists of two rounds. First round being a 'unusual' treasure hunt where QR codes need to be scanned and the clues will be in different foreign or Indian languages, participants should figure out the clue and reach the next location. Along with the clue for the location, they'll get two more clues that will be the conflicting problem statements. 

In round 2, participants need to come up with only one solution that relates both the conflicting statements.

ROUND 1

A QR Code based treasure hunt.

A initial location is required where we give instruction to the participants and also give them the first clue.

The first clue will have 3 statements/clues, in which one of it will lead them to the next location and the the other two are the clues for two conflicting problem statements.

Like this the treasure hunt continues for 7-8 locations, in each location they'll get three clues, out of which one of it will be the clue for the next location and the two will be clues for conflicting problem statements.

The clues will be encrypted in any Indian or foreign languages (like German, Chinese, etc)

They have to figure out what the clue is, using google or any mode. 

Once they figure out the clue they have to go to that location and tell what language their previous clue was in. 

ROUND 2

All the teams should figure out what the two conflicting problem statements are using the clues that they've collected.

Once they figure out, they have to come up with a single solution that solves both the conflicting statements.

Team Size : 2-4 members:

Mock Placement

What is Mock Placement ?

Mock Placement is a simulation of an actual Placement drive. Actual placement procedures might vary from company to company but the mock placement gives a general idea of how the process might take place.

Why Mock Placement ?

Mock Placement provides an opportunity to become familiar with the procedure in an environment similar to that of actual placement. A detailed review would be provided at the end highlighting the strengths and underlining the flaws.

The event will take place in three rounds :

Round 1 :
Aptitude exam

Round 2 :
Group Discussion

Round 3 :
2 Rounds of Interviews (Technical and TechnoManagerial)
