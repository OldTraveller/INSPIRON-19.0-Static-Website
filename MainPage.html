About UVCE

Being the 5th Engineering College in the country, established in the year 1917 by Bharat Ratna Sir M.Visvesvaraya, UVCE has acquired a leading position in technical education. Currently affiliated to Bangalore University, the college provides 6 B.E courses and 22 M.Tech courses. UVCE provides an opportunity to excel and assists in all-round development for a student through various channels and platforms. Situated at the heart of Bengaluru, UVCE has a rich network of notable alumni who occupy important positions not only within the country but also abroad.

About TPO

The Training & Placement Office was started in 1989 to facilitate campus recruitment for UVCE students. It is a mission to act as a liaison between the industry and students in order to fulfill their aspiration in securing the right job where they can learn and use their knowledge to contribute towards the development of the industry and our society. On an average, about 150 companies visit the campus for recruitment processes. Besides this, Training and Placement Office also conducts various activities for enabling students to develop their overall personality by training them in technical and soft skills. In addition to the training and placement activities, TPO also organizes the annual inter-collegiate techno-management extravaganza - INSPIRON.

About INSPIRON

Since its inception in 2009, INSPIRON has been hosted by the Training & Placement Office, UVCE. With INSPIRON, UVCE provides its students an opportunity to hone as well as showcase their practical know - how by organizing events that focus on all the key areas for personal development of a student. In addition to testing the domain knowledge and awareness about the theme of the students, the events are designed to help them improve their communication and marketing skills as well. This intercollegiate techno - management extravaganza -INSPIRON from UVCE has been very well received and has attracted a lots of student crowd over the past years.

Dr. HN Ramesh - Principal UVCE and Dr. BM Rajaprakash - Training & Placement Officer UVCE, cordially invite all engineering students to come and participate in the Annual Techno-Manegerial fest of UVCE - INSPIRON 19.0
