Points System

Below is a points system metric that will be functional for the duration of Inspiron 19.0

# Participation Type Points Rewarded
1 Registration for a MAIN event +5
2 Winners of a MAIN event +4
3 Runners Up of a MAIN event +2
4 Winner of point boosters +3

NOTE

  • The points for registering for an event are automatically added to your USN the moment you register.
  • The person at the top of the leaderboard, at the end of 2nd day of INSPIRON (30th Novemeber 2019) will receive a cash prize of 1000 for active participations and/or winnings.
  • In case of a tie between a set of people, the prize money allocated for that position will be shared between the number of people having a tie.
  • Point Boosters will be conducted in between the fest, where you can always increase your points and get higher rank on the LeaderBoard.
  • "MAIN Event" refers to the events that are directly shown on the Events tab of the website.
  • Each participant should register to one or more events with exactly one USN to maintain their scores in the LeaderBoard.
  • The sole descrition of alloting the points lies in the hands of the Inspiron Organizers.
  • To claim your points for the winning of events, you have to report to one of the Organizer with a valid identity to avail the points.
